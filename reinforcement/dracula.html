Dracula rule

Background

Dracula is attacking suburban Transylvania. People are hurrying to try and grab mirrors. Can you find the area of your mirror to see if it's big enough to reflect sunlight at Dracula? You grab your handy measuring tape to measure the area of your mirror, but your mirror is irregularly shaped! How do you know how big it is? The Trapezoid Rule is a method of estimating the area of an irregular shape given a set amount of measurements of the length across it at regular intervals. Given measurements A B C D E F, the formula is 1/2 times (A + 2B +2C +2D +2E +F). In other words, double all the values except the first and last, and divide the total by 2.

Input Expectations

Each line of input will be an integer. The number of lines is unspecified, but will be fewer than twenty. The last input will be zero.

Sample Input

0
5
3
6
3
4
0

Output Expectations

The output will be a single floating point value.

Sample Output

15.0
